12th April, 2024

Educational Workshop at GSSS, Domehar

On April 12, 2024, the Koshish Club of Jaypee Youth Club at Jaypee University of Information Technology organized an Educational Workshop at Government Senior Secondary School, Domehar, under the guidance of faculty coordinator Dr. Raj Kumar. A team of student volunteers, including Vidushi, Niharika, Palak Sharma, Nikhilesh, Nitish Gupta, Anjali Panwar, Dhriti Sharma, Vanshaj Sharma, Vardaan, and Aastha Jain, focused on teaching basic mathematics to students in classes 1st to 5th and Vedic mathematics techniques to those in classes 6th to 8th. By tailoring the workshop to specific age groups, the club aimed to enhance learning and empower underprivileged children with essential skills. The event highlighted the club’s mission to provide quality education while inspiring others to contribute. It also offered valuable experience to the volunteers, fostering both immediate and long-term impact on educational empowerment.

19th - 20th March, 2024 by admin

Colours of Giving

Koshish Club organized its first donation drive, Colours of Giving, with the aim of spreading joy to underprivileged children, inspired by the spirit of Holi. The drive encouraged contributions from the university community in the form of stationery, clothes, or monetary support to benefit children from localities, worker families, or others in need. Funds raised were used to purchase gifts, which were personally distributed to ensure they reached the intended beneficiaries.The event's success was driven by faculty coordinators Dr. Shikha Mittal and Dr. Raj Kumar, along with dedicated student coordinators and volunteers. Colours of Giving highlighted the power of collective kindness, fostering compassion and community support.
